Welcome to Web 1

This course is an introduction to web development using HTML and CSS, as well as other languages and programs. The course will consist of lectures, in-class demonstrations, studio work and group work. We will explore the use of the Internet, multimedia and software as a platform for art and design. Technical and conceptual aspects will be combined in the production of web based work.

Check this site for updates to weekly reading and assignments, as well as video demonstrations and lecture notes.
