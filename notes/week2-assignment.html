Add CSS to week 1 assignment

  • This should be pretty straight forward.
  • Add a style sheet to your website structure.
  • Define the basic CSS styles: html, body, p, h1, ul, li, etc.
  • Create at least three classes and assign them styles.
  • Create at least three IDs and assign the styles.
  • Research styles on W3Schools and other CSS sites. Look at font styles, background color, etc.
  • Add a background image or color or gradient to your site.
  • Style your hyperlinks using pseudo classes -- a:link, a:visited, a:hover and a:active.
  • Upload homework to week2 in your server.
