Make a site with a two column layout

This week we are going to start making new sites. If you want to continue developing your site from Week 1 and 2, I am happy to look at it and give you feedback. But this week we are starting from scratch. We're going to focus on layout so we need some info. Choose a person to make a site about. It could be a famous person like an actor, athlete, writer or just someone you know like your grandma. Make a site with information about them and some images. Your layout should have two columns, a header and a navigation menu, footer, or both. Arrange the information and media about this person in a way that makes sense to the user. You should have more than one page.

If you can't think of someone to make a site about, let me suggest my favorite basketball player, Rasheed Wallace, or the great poet W. B. Yeats. There's a ton of information available online about both.
