Final project wireframes

This week we're making wire frames for our final projects. These mockups should be comprehensive. Use whatever style of wireframe you prefer, but they should describe every page, layout, piece of content and hover state or other pseudo states. Include some of the media assets (photos, videos, graphics) that will appear in your final site.

Grading will be based on completeness and overall quality in these apects:

  • Page layouts
  • Color scheme
  • Inclusions of media assets
  • Typography
