Final presentation

Each final presentation should be 5-10 minutes in length and should demonstrate the functionality and usage of the final project website. It should cover the design and development process, including uses of JavaScript and JavaScript libraries and other resources, including HTML/CSS or digital media work, depending on the project. The final should also cover the basic elements of design that were considered in developing the site, including form, color and typography. The content of the site and it's relationship to the design and functionality should also be discussed.
