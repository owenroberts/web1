Final wireframes and project plan

Over the next few weeks we will do in class workshops on the final projects. I will meet with each of you individually each week to look at progress and help with technical and conceptual questions.

This week map out the elements of your project, documents, code, libraries, image assets and other content, and make a basic structure for completing each task over the end of the semester. We will use that as an outline to complete the projects.

Along with the project plan, make some visual wireframes that will layout the basic design for you project. This can be done with HTML/CSS or in Photoshop/Illustrator, or another graphics program. You should have dummy content in place of all of the image, text or video assets you plan to use, along with a defined format, color scheme, typographic scheme and page layout. Have a few different iterations of your idea to look at next week.
